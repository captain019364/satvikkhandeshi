📖 Our Story

From a Small Kitchen to Maharashtra's Heart

Satvik Khandeshi was born out of a simple passion: to bring the authentic flavours of Khandesh to every home. Proprietor Vilas S. Bhirud started this journey over 15 years ago in a small kitchen in Dombivli, inspired by generations of family recipes passed down through the rolling fields of Khandesh.

What began as a home-based venture quickly grew into a beloved local brand. Today, with shops in Thakurli and Dombivli, we serve thousands of happy customers — students, families, and office workers — who count on us for their daily dose of crispy, wholesome snacks.

Every product we make is a tribute to tradition: slow-roasted grains, pure desi ghee, and spice blends that carry the soul of our homeland.

🎯

Our Mission

To make traditional Khandeshi flavours accessible, affordable, and consistently delicious for every household.

💚

Our Values

Authenticity, purity, hygiene, and respect for our culinary heritage guide every decision we make.

🏅

Quality Commitment

All snacks are prepared fresh daily in a hygienic, certified kitchen using natural ingredients — zero compromises.
